About Me

I love Spreading joy to kids through music

Jessie, also known as JJ, is a mom of two littles with a passion for music. She loves taking her children to local library music events and broadway shows. With many nieces and nephews, she has been entertaining large groups of young children for years. After performing at local libraries and kid events, Jessie has found a new passion - performing interactive live music for kids! Her favorite part is watching kids light up when engaging in the creativity and excitement of live music. Those moments are incredibly special.
